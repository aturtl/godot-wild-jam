GODOT WILD JAM DESIGN LAYOUT

Theme: Mutation
Wildcards: Disassembly, Checkout, Turtle

Idea: Robot turtle assembly
	Mutation: Mech structure of turtle mutates based on how you take care of it
	Disassembly: Repurpose parts from disassembled robots
	Checkout: Purchase parts from shop for turtle
	Turtle: Assemble robot turtle

Basic Mechanics:
	Assembly
		Nodes to attach parts
		Parts give unique modifiers
	Shop: currency, shop layout
		Menus
	Battle
		The turtle walks in random directions & flings itself at the enemy
			Like duck life. You build it then watch it autonomously.
			For interactivity, projectiles/other weapons could be timed by the player.
		The head is the main damage area. The shell resists the most.
	Attachments
		The parts attached to the turtle chasis
		Weapons:
			Shell-top, Tail
		Protection:
			Shell, Head
		Mobility:
			Legs
		Should mainly follow this but some attachments can have multiple purposes. The head, for example, could have a laser attachment to it.

Attachments:
	Mace Tail - Chained to tail, swings freely with gravity
